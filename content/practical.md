# Who can participate?

- Any student, PhD Student, staff member or professor in Computer Science of Hasselt University
- Any student in the Industrial Engineering: Computer Science and Electronics-IT department of Hasselt University

# When? Where?

14 May 2025, 12:00 - 20:00

Agora @ Hasselt University

We will take care of catering. Drinks and dinner will be provided.

Thanks to our sponsors, this event is free for you to join.

# Teams

You participate in teams. Teams must have 3 or 4 members, but you can freely mix between years/fields of study. You will participate in the highest category of your team members.

# What do you need?

To participate, 
- All team members have registered at **CTFd** and joined the team. You have to bring your own laptop.
- If you want to, you can have a look at the [Tips](/tips)-page for some preparation that you can do.
- A **wired network connection** will be required to complete the challenges! We will bring the switches and a few patch cables, but feel free to bring your own cable. - Also, if your laptop does not have an Ethernet (RJ45) connector and you have a dongle, don't forget it! We will provide a few of those as well.
